# NextStep — Project Brain

## Mission
Empower every high school student with an AI-powered academic companion that simplifies grades, planning, and college readiness — all in one app.

## The Problem We Solve
- Grades are scattered across outdated portals (HAC, Skyward, PowerSchool)
- Planners are not personalized to the student
- Students don't understand how today's choices affect GPA, graduation, and college readiness
- Counselors are stretched thin (500+ students each) — proactive guidance is missing

## Target Users
- Primary: High school students (grades 9–12)
- Secondary: Parents / guardians
- Tertiary: School counselors (dashboard access, Phase 4)

## Core MVP Features (Phase 1 — 6–12 months)
1. **Grade Viewer** — Integrates with HAC, Skyward, PowerSchool. Transcript & report card preview.
2. **AI-Powered GPA Simulator** — "What-if" GPA scenarios. College readiness predictions.
3. **Smart Planner** — Syncs with Canvas / Google Classroom. AI organizes assignments into daily/weekly study plans.
4. **High School Roadmap** — 9th–12th grade course suggestions. Tracks graduation requirements & progress. College prep guidance.

## Monetization
- Free tier: core grade viewing + basic planner
- Premium tier (Phase 3): advanced GPA/college predictor, AI study tips, career pathway suggestions
- Family Bundle: multi-student household plan
- School licensing: per-seat B2B contracts (Phase 3+)

## Business Roadmap Summary
| Phase | Timeline | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| 0 | 0–6 mo | Market validation, 1,000+ signups, integration feasibility |
| 1 | 6–12 mo | MVP: Grade Viewer + GPA Simulator beta (2–3 schools) |
| 2 | 12–24 mo | Smart Planner + HS Roadmap, expand to 5–10 schools |
| 3 | 24–36 mo | Premium tier, Family Bundle, school licensing, 10k users |
| 4 | 36–48 mo | College Readiness Suite, counselor dashboards, 50+ districts |

## Compliance Requirements (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- **FERPA** — Student education records are protected. Never expose PII without consent.
- **COPPA** — Users under 13 require verifiable parental consent.
- **State privacy laws** — Vary by district; legal review required before launch ($3k–$7.5k budget).
- All student data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Consent flow must be attorney-reviewed before any beta launch.
